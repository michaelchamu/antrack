Ludwig Kapingana

Executive: Commercial Business Unit

Ludwig heads the Commercial Business Unit and is responsible for the improvement of our presence in the downstream sector. As a business executive, he provides sound operational solutions custom-made for the needs of our customers and aims to position the Commercial Business Unit favourably.

He is also responsible for developing competitive advantage within the market as well as implementing policies i.e. operational strategies, business plans, structures, systems and capabilities.

He is also in charge of the development and management of all reimgs within the statutory requirements framework.

Ludwig holds a Sales and Marketing Certificate from Damelin as well as a Trades Certificate from the Johannesburg Stock Exchange.

Bonifatius Konjore

Executive: Information Communications Technology

Bonifatius heads our ICT Unit and is responsible for the management and direction of all ICT reimgs in line with our business strategy and priorities. He is a University of Cape Town graduate where he obtained a Bachelor of Science Degree in Computer and Statistics.

He also possesses 18 years working experience in telecommunications, Government and utilities sector.

He further has a broad technical background, an accomplished and talented information technology professional with project management experience and strong background of Business systems.

Julia Muetudhana

Executive: Commercial Business Unit

Julia holds several degrees in Industrial Psychology from the University of Stellenbosch as well as a Masters in Business Administration Degree from ESAMI. She is a certified Talent Economist.

Her key responsibilities include determining and refining our Human Capital & Strategic Development areas, with a particular emphasis on formulation and implementation of Human Reimg Strategy, Policies, Procedures, Governance and statutory demands.

She is also responsible for establishing best practices within the industry and to implement the value chain of HR that consist of performance management, employee relations, remuneration, training and development and organisational development.

She is also a former member of the Human Reimgs sub-committee of the Namibia Students Financial Assistance Fund (NSFAF).
