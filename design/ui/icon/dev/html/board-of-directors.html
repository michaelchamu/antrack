Johannes !Gawaxab

Chairperson

Johannes !Gawaxab was appointed Chairman of the Board in January 2012, and was reappointed as Chairman in September 2013. The Harvard University Advanced Management Programme graduate is the Managing Director of Old Mutual, Africa Operations.

Johannes holds a Masters degree in Business Leadership from the University of South Africa, as well as a Masters of Arts degree from the Graduate School of Business at Kingston in London. He also has a Post Graduate Diploma in Leadership from London Business School.

Other directorships & Offices

Apart from serving on the board of Old Mutual Namibia, Johannes serves as a director on the boards of Old Mutual Malawi, Kenya, Nigeria, Ghana and Zimbabwe.

 
 
Ally Angula

Vice: Chairperson

Ally Angula is a Chattered Accountant registered with both SAICA in South Africa and ICAN in Namibia. She was admitted to the KPMG Partnership in Namibia as an Assurance Partner on 1 September 2006 and retired from KPMG in 2013, February to form a company called Leap Holdings (Pty) Ltd (Formerly Leap Investments (Pty) Ltd.

Other Directorships & Offices

In addition to being the Vice Chairperson of the Namcor Board, Ally serves as non-executive director for Old Mutual Life Assurance (Namibia), Rio Tinto’s Rossing Uranium mine in Namibia, Oryx Properties Limited (a NSX primary listed company) and the Bank of Namibia.

Ally served as a Council Member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants in Namibia (ICAN) and as a Board member of the National Qualifications Authority appointed by the Minister of Education.

Besides being Leap Holdings’ Managing and Strategic Director, Ally was appointed by the Ministry of Finance to oversee the reform of public expenditure management and financial reporting.

Ally is the Founding Curator for the World Economic Forum Global Shapers Windhoek Hub as well as an Archbishop Desmond Tutu Fellow.

 
 
Anna Libana

Director

Anna Libana was appointed to the Board in October 2010 and was reappointed in October 2013. She is the Deputy Director: National Energy Fund in the Ministry of Mines & Energy, and is busy finalizing a Master of International Business with the Polytechnic of Namibia.

Anna has a Bachelor of Commerce from the University of South Africa and a National Diploma in Public Administration from the Polytechnic of Namibia.

 
 
Gottlieb Amanyanga

Director

Gottlieb Nendongo Amanyanga was appointed to the Board in October 2013. He is a Chemical Engineer by profession, and is the current Chief Executive Officer of the Northern Regional Electricity Distribution Company (NORED).

He holds a Masters degree in Chemical Engineering from Burgas University of Technology in Bulgaria, and a Diploma in Petroleum Engineering from the Institute of Chemical Technology also in Bulgaria.

He is currently finalizing an executive Master of Business Administration (MBA) with the Eastern & Southern Africa Management Institute (ESAMI).

Other Directorships & Offices

Gottlieb is no first timer to the NAMCOR Board, having had a year long stint on the institution’s board from the year 2004 to 2005.

He is a past board member of Namibia’s national power utility NAMPOWER, as well as a past member of the Ongwediva College of Education. He is Namibia’s representative on the World Energy Council.

 
 
Merceline Geises

Director

Merceline Mercia Geises was appointed to the Board in October 2013. She is a Legal Practitioner by profession, and is Old Mutual Namibia’s Head of Alternative Investments.

She has a Masters in Business Administration from the University of Stellenbosch, a Masters degree in Mercantile Law and an LLB degree – all from the University of Free State in South Africa.
